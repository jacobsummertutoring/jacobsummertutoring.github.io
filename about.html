My name is Jacob, and I recently graduated from Purdue University with a B.S. in Applied Physics Honors, Mathematics, and Computer Science. I have a passion for teaching and tutoring. I have several years of experience as a private tutor, and I have also worked as a teaching assistant for introductory physics at Purdue.

About the Program

Jacob's Summer Tutoring is designed to provide support and resources for students enrolled in UC Scout's Geometry course. The program cost is $150 per semester, which includes access to all posted resources, access to all group meetings (3 1-hour session per week), and two 30-minute one-on-one sessions per semester. Weekly group meetings are designed to help build understanding while also keeping students on track with the course material. During these sessions, I will review the material covered in the course, work through practice problems, and answer any questions students may have.I will be posting typed notes and resources throughout the course, and I am available for tutoring sessions to help students understand the material and succeed in the course. Additional one-on-one sessions can be scheduled for $20 per session. Please see the contact section for more information on how to get in touch and schedule sessions.

In addition to Geometry, I am also happy to assist students with other subjects or areas where they may need support. Please feel free to reach out with any questions or to schedule a tutoring session.

Subjects I Can Assist With

  • UC Scout's Geometry course
  • Mathematics [Algebra, Precalculus, Calculus, Linear Algebra, Differential Equations]
  • Physics [Algebra-based Physics, Calculus-based Mechanics, Electricity & Magnetism, Introduction to Quantum Mechanics]
  • Computer Science [Introductory Programming, Introduction to Python, and Introduction to Programming with C]
